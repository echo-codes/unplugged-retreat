Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat

There are shuttles that will take you and your team from the airport to the retreat. There are also limited parking options for small charter buses and vans.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

ALL teams are welcome to take part in our program.

What size teams can you accommodate?

We can accommodate team sizes ranging from 6 to 20. Larger groups are asked to RSVP at least a month or two in advance so that we can make sure we are fully staffed and prepared (which includes having enough food on hand, so you'll want to make sure you let us know).

Is there really no wifi available?

We have internet hookups located in our meeting rooms. However, the use of these hookups are determined by your appointed team leader and must be cleared with Unplugged staff beforehand. We truly believe that disconnecting from the real world allows you to focus on what really matters.
